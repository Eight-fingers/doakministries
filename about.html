About the ministry

Built from survival, shaped by faith, and dedicated to restoration.

Daughter of a King Inc was not built from a business plan. It was built from survival. That truth continues to shape the heart, tone, and purpose of everything we do.

Our story

A ministry born from lived experience.

Jasmine Sanchez knows what it feels like to sit in silence while abuse is happening behind closed doors. She knows what addiction can do to a life. She knows what trauma can do to the mind, body, and faith.

Daughter of a King Inc was created so women do not have to heal alone. The ministry exists to offer support, community, and a faith-based path toward healing for women who have survived domestic violence, narcissistic abuse, addiction, and trauma.

What this ministry is

  • A place for restoration
  • A place rooted in biblical truth
  • A place for trauma-informed support
  • A place where women do not have to pretend

Mission

Helping women break cycles of abuse, reclaim identity, and walk in the authority and dignity God intended.

We believe healing is possible. We believe women deserve safe support, honest compassion, and a community that points them toward truth, hope, and restoration.

Founder and Executive Director

Jasmine Sanchez

Jasmine Sanchez is the Founder and Executive Director of Daughter of a King Inc. Her heart for this work is deeply personal. Out of her own journey of survival and restoration, she is building a ministry that offers women the kind of support, understanding, and faith-filled encouragement she knows they need.

Her vision is to create safe spaces where women can find advocacy resources, healing community, and a renewed understanding of who they are in Christ.

If you are a survivor, you are welcome here.

If you want to support women rebuilding their lives, you are welcome here.

If you are looking for a community that believes healing is possible, you are welcome here.
